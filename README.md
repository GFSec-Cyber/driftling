# Driftling 🌊

A bioluminescent underwater arcade game for iOS.

Tap to float. Dodge the coral pillars. See how far you can drift.

## Features

- 13 unique ocean characters to collect and unlock
- 12 stunning underwater worlds
- 27 trophies and achievements
- Daily bonus coins
- Level up your drifter
- Unlock rare characters through skill and persistence

## Support

Having an issue or question? We'd love to help.

**Email:** GFSec-Cyber@proton.me

Common issues:
- **Game won't load** — try restarting the app or reinstalling from the App Store
- **Progress lost** — progress is saved locally on your device; deleting the app removes save data
- **Coins not appearing** — exit to the main menu and re-enter the game to refresh

---

## Privacy Policy

Driftling does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The game saves your progress locally on your device only. No accounts, no tracking, no ads, no internet connection required to play.

For questions about privacy, contact: GFSec-Cyber@proton.me
